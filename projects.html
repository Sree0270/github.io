I am an Assistant Professor in the College of Information Sciences and Technology at Pennsylvania State University.

Research Interests: Specifically, my work (1) addresses emerging privacy issues using a mixed-method approach to understand people and develop solutions grounded in empirical evidence; and (2) develops methods and tools for engaging with diverse stakeholders at scale to design privacy and governance solutions.
A few directions of interest include:
- Privacy and security solutions for marginalized and underserved communities
- Human-in-the-loop [deliberative] governance for LLMs
- Usable security and privacy for Web3/DeFi

Prospective Students: I’m looking for PhD students to join my group. If you are interested in working with me, please send me an email with your CV.
